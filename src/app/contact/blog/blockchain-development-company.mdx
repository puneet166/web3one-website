# Blockchain Development Cost

Blockchain development costs vary depending on the complexity of the project and the technologies used.

## Factors Affecting Blockchain Development Cost

### Project Complexity

Large blockchain systems with multiple smart contracts and infrastructure layers require more development time.

### Smart Contract Development

Smart contract complexity influences development cost significantly.

### Security Audits

Professional security audits are essential for blockchain applications.

### Infrastructure Requirements

Enterprise-grade blockchain systems require scalable cloud infrastructure.

## Estimated Cost Ranges

* **Smart contracts:** moderate cost  
* **DeFi platforms:** higher cost  
* **NFT marketplaces:** moderate to high cost  

## FAQ

### Why is blockchain development expensive?

Blockchain development requires specialized expertise and extensive security testing.

### Can startups afford blockchain development?

Yes, startups often begin with MVPs and scale gradually.

## Conclusion

Blockchain development costs depend on project requirements, security standards, and platform complexity.
